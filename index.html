About Project

A Deep Dive into Airline Status Analysis

Here we analyze an airline dataset to provide the current status of arrivals, departures, and on-time performance of flights based on previous years' travelers' data.

Basically, our project is based on a sample dataset of airline status analysis. It helps travelers find the accurate timings of flights. It gives a brief information of flight status and provides services to the travelers.
